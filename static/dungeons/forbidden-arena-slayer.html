Luminous / Dungeons / Forbidden Arena [Hagufna]

Forbidden arena is a single boss with multiple small phases based on health %.
This is mostly a reactionary dungeon with a little bit of scripted events and its about learning each combo on its own.

Debuffs

Icon Effect
Getting hit by most of his attacks will apply a 60 second debuff that stacks 4 times. This debuff does nothing until it reaches 4 stacks.
Once debuff above reaches 4, it will change into a different debuff for 30 seconds that doubles damage taken from the boss.
This increases your damage taken by x3 for 5 seconds, its removed if you retaliate instantly


Leaping Strike:

Double upwards slash into Overhand Strike

The boss will slash twice in an upwards motion followed by an Overhand strike.
Overhand strike is not blockable and must be iframed.
If you get knocked down, a debuff stack will be applied (debuff-3)

If you do not retaliate immediately, the boss will trigger a leaping strike on you as an execution, you will not survive. Its a one shot.
● Taldekk only survives because of passive.


Upward + Downward slash into Eviscerate

Similar to overhand with a slight difference, his second slash will have a downwards angle. All hits are blockable.
Eviscerate will knockdown and apply Weakness exposed(debuff-3) if not blocked.


Upward slashes into backstep into random Overhand Strike

Exactly the same as the first combo but now the boss will backstep and then randomly target someone for an overhand strike.
The backstep is particularly annoying because he will iframe all your skills during it.


90% Phase Change + Shield

At 90% the boss will say Pathetic fools...you asked for it and change phase.
He will gain a buff for the rest of the fight that increases his damage by 10% and attack speed by 5%

Failing to break this shield will result in him doing an AOE within 25 meters for massive damage. It can be tanked with damage reduction skills or blue shield only.
The shield will happen at 90%, 60%, 30% OR every 90 seconds if you dont push % fast enough.

When you fail to break the shield, he will gain a stack of Condensed Darkness, increasing his damage by 10% per stack.

Buffs

Icon Effect
This isnt a big deal unless you fail to break the shield 10 times in a row. If he gets 10 stacks of this buff, he will gain Bloodlust.
At 10stacks,this increases his damage by 5x, move speed by 50%, and attack speed by 20%. This forces you to fully wipe and reset.

85% Pull in Mechanic

Starting at 85% and then about every minute after, the boss will hold his sword straight up in the air and begin channeling energy into it, either pink or purple energy.
This will then pull everyone within 25m right on top of him and then do a mechanic based on which energy color it was.The pull in is not avoidable in any way aside from outranging it.
Pay close attention to his sword, around the hilt area to tell which color energy it is.

Pink Energy:

After the pull, he will stab his sword into the ground creating 3 shockwaves. These waves deal massive damage but are survivable with damage reduction/shields.
If by chance you do survive a wave, you will be silenced for 5 seconds and knocked down. Because you are a silenced you cannot retal. The boss will auto target anyone silenced with an overhand strike, and if by chance you survive that a leaping strike.

Purple Energy:

After being pulled, the boss will do a fast spin that applies bleeding (cleanseable) if hit directly by spin.
The spin will also create a shockwave traveling outwards that stuns anyone hit. This wave is iframeable.
Finally the boss will charge an unsheathe after the quick spin. The general idea is to escape quickly as you get pulled, dodging the stun wave as you go.
Fail and you get stunned inside the unsheathe, at least it looks cool when you die.

Red Lines

Also starting at 90% are random red lines. These deal about 100k damage and apply a crippling slow if hit.
-60% move speed, -70% attack speed, and -90% healing for 6 seconds.


These lines are iframeable but often come at the worst times possible.
● 1 at a time will happen starting at 90%
● 2 at a time will happen starting at 30%
Each has their own cooldown so a total of 3 at once can happen if unlucky.



After 85% the boss gains a few new combos

Heart Thrust combo

The boss will stab the tank with heart thrust, do a turning slash, and then overhand strike directly behind him. The heart thrust and turn slash are blockable.
The killer here is the small turning slash he does, mostly gets dps gets hit by this. As it applies a debuff leading to 4 stacks.


Double Spin into Overhand

The boss will do a whirlwind twice followed by an overhand strike on the tank. The spins are blockable.
The spins hit hard and apply debuffs - most important to avoid as dps/healers
Standing on the bosses left side as a dps/healer avoids you getting hit by spins most of the time, super helpful.


Triple Spin into Punishing Blow

A variation on the previous move is this: the boss will instead spin 3 times total and then do a punishing blow attack.
All 3 spins are blockable and the jumping attack must be perfect blocked if tank, iframed if dps/healer.
There are only 2 situations where the boss will use punishing blow and one of them is this, only if he triple spins.


Upward slashes into Overhand into Punishing Blow

A variation on an earlier combo of upwards slashx2 into overhand strike on tank. This time it will be followed up by a punishing blow.




This is the only other time he will use punishing blow.

60% Shield

Same as earlier, shield of 400 million HP.


Range check / Ress bait

At any point after 85%, if a player gets a certain distance away from the boss OR if anyone casts a ressurection spell/scroll, the boss will target them.
These have their own separate cooldowns and can trigger back to back and often do because of how the mechanic works.

The boss will target the person and use savage strike to them, often through/behind them and then overhand strike. People often panic and use their iframe to dodge the dash itself and get hit by the overhand strike.
The dash hitbox is very narrow, narrow enough that simply walking sideways will dodge it, save your iframe for the overhand strike.



55% phase change

The boss will say My patience is wearing thin... refreshing his damage increase buff and unlocking more combos

Double Savage Strike

This attack ruins dps lives.
The boss will do a quick turning slash and Savage strike directly away from the main aggro target, turn slash, Savage strike back to the tank, and finally overhand strike the tank.
Thats a total of 3 stacks if dps dont move in time from the turnslash and both savage strikes.
It will ALWAYS go directly away from the aggro target and always return to that same aggro target.


Spinning stun combo

There are 2 variations to this and its entirely reactionary for the tank.
● The boss will do a small spin and hold in place for a second.Then he will either attempt to stun you followed by an overhand strike.
OR
● He will fake you out, not casting the stun and simply overhanding you.

The stun is blockable so thats not the issue.

30% final phase

When reaching 30%, the boss will say Thats Enough! and charge a giant aoe.
You can see the edge of it if you are zoomed out far enough. When it is near completion, iframe.
The aoe can also be survived if you have damage reduction or blue shield.
At the same time, he will trigger a separate set of double red line, independant of the single red line from 90%.
The aoe will kill you, the lines shouldnt.
You should get another shield right when this mechanic ends.

The boss will gain his final form, a new buff called called Mad Power, increasing his damage by 15% and attack speed by 5%
Hewill also unlock his final combo, ontop of everything listed above

Buff

Icon Effect
Increases damage by 15% and attack speed by 5%

Tank Buster Combo

The boss will charge up in place for a second followed by an extended combo only under 30%.
The boss will do this in order:
● Double spin
● Overhand strike (will be canceled if red eye flash)
● Eviscerate
● Overhand strike
There will be zero delay between them.If you get hit by the first overhand strike, you will take x3 damage from the eviserate and INSTANTLY die.
Luckily this combo is easily avoided as a tank.If you iframe to the side after the double spin, he will be locked on your position and simply miss the entire rest of the combo.



Doing this correctly, you should be hitting the boss on the side while he misses you entirely.
If you dont position correctly the boss will move forward with his attacks a good distance, often triggering a range check on dps.
