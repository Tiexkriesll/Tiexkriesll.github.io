Tenacity / Dungeons / Shadow Sanguinary

Dungeon is exactly the same like old versions, but easier since its return.

First Boss | Pahoegar



Barrage

Boss will take his coomer arm and swing it left and right rapidly, pushing tank back.
Hold block for this.


4 Hit Combo

All 4 hits of this are blockable, hits 3 and 4 hit behind as well but doesnt matter in this version.


Lasers

The boss will select one target and shoot a laser at them. This generally isnt fatal at 520 ilvl, instead should just be focused on not moving the boss and killing the pillars when available.
The timing for this is to watch his small hand, he will curl his fingers and then begin uncurling.
The moment he begins uncurling his fingers press iframe.

Single laser

He can target the same person over and over again without limit:

Pillars

The boss will spawn purple orbs above dps (usually) along with a giant aura on himself.
After some seconds, pillars will spawn on the spot where people with the purple orb were standing.

These should be dropped behind the boss, perferably a tiny bit away from the boss, as these will split your damage and make the runs slower. Take a second and back up 5m away from the boss to drop these together, its worth.
Pillars can be destroyed by aiming a boss laser at them, thats why you want them grouped up nice and tight.

Not like this, this will ruin your dps:

Pillars spam harmless aoes.

Shield

As long as one pillar remains active, the boss can trigger a shield. He will run to the pillar and kneel down for 10 seconds. Failing to break this results in a wipe but if you have 520 ilvl, there is a zero % chance you fail this.


AoE on floor

Boss will kneel and do a series of 8 aoes on the floor.
They are entirely harmless at 520 ilvl so you dont have to worry about them.


Second Boss | Dakuryon

This boss has only 2 major mechanics worth mentioning, absolutely nothing is fatal. The only annoying part of this boss is wasting your time with immune phases, which can be skipped with patience at the start.

95% Immunity and Adds

At 95%, the boss will gain a shield, making him take VERY little damage and spawning harmless adds.
Tanks, your job here is to bring the archer to your left next to boss for cleave.

You must wait until he fires 3 lasers at a random single target for the phase to end.
DO NOT use your dps cooldowns until this is over


90% Pizza Time

At 90%, the boss will move to the center and begin spawning pizzas.
In this version of the dungeon, these are ENTIRELY ignoreable, afk dps even at the entry level of 520 ilvl.

Healers use kaia and heal but largely just afk dps



The goal here is to chain his pizza phases back to back, which you should have no trouble doing if you didnt burn before 90%.
If you dont properly chain them together, he will enter more immune phases as it was meant to alternate phases = immune -pizza - immune - pizza


20% Burn Phase

At 20% the boss will run to the giant crystal on the wall and begin channeling, gaining a buff ticking down from 20 seconds.
You have to get him to 10% before he wipes you.
In this version of the dungeon, buff can also run out but he still takes longer than that to wipe you, again afk dps


Last Boss | Imperator

Firstly, turn your tera volume down, because the bosses first action in combat to utterly EARRAPE you with poor audio mixing, screaming I WILL CRUSH YOU

At start of battle, he will do a big outer AoE. Go near him. Thats all.
This boss can and should be rez baited off cooldown, even while walking in at start.
Healer should rez bait before even doing this engage attack, keep him animation locked healers.




Ress bait / Ranged attack

A larger stationary circle followed by a smaller tracking circle. Walk out of the first one and wait for the small circle to vanish, then iframe.
These can and should be chained back to back while behind the tank.




Swipes

The boss will do a series of 3 swipes out of a total possible 4.
Front, Back, Left, or Right, any of the 3 and never repeating.
The boss 2 unique animations for these.

If he slams his sword Straight:

He will either swipe to the left OR backwards





In this example he does:

Curved (front)
Curved ( right) (must be right because only option left)
Straight (left) (could be left or back)


If the sword is curved:

He will either swipe to the right OR front




In this example he does:

Curved (front)
Straight ( left)
Straight (back) (only option left)


Adds and Cage

The boss will remain still, charging up an animation while summoning adds. The lower hp the boss is, the more adds that will spawn.
If left alive, these adds will mutate into monsters and begin spawning red balls all over the room. In this version of the dungeon these are largely harmless.

Once you get a bit of gear or are confident, these can be ignored entirely. The boss will then spawn a cage, which you must be in, or you instantly die.

The hits are: Pizza 1, pizza 2, in, out, giant pizza. Hits 1-4 are semi random but hit 5 is always a large pizza


While you cannot face tank cage, you can iframe each part.

Here is another example with lower boss hp so more adds to kill.

The safe spots for all of these remains entirely static based on how the boss is facing.
These clips have people wiggling around and you do NOT need to move at all.


This position is safe from every single hit, except the inner hit which you can iframe.This is slightly off cardinal position but you get the idea. It will look like this:

● Pizza 2 (you are safe)
● Inner hit (iframe it)

Heres an example:

Under 35% Cage

When the boss is under 35%, he will now sometimes give people a red skull debuff with stacks ranging from 1-4
This requires you to get hit by the cage 1-4 times to remove it. If not removed you will instantly die.


Shield

Originally this happened at 70% and 50% but it will be pushed back or not happen at all in this version with good dps.
The boss will target the aggro target with an eye, stunning them after 5 or so seconds.

If you have this eye, run a little bit away.
The boss will then pursue you while shielding, break the shield before he reaches his shield target.


Double sword slam + Closest target

Boss will slam the handle of his sword on the ground twice, the first hit will pull anyone hit to him.
After the second slam he will target the closest player to him with a debuff
Taking this debuff twice in a row will stun you for a VERY long time. This was fatal in the past but not anymore.
