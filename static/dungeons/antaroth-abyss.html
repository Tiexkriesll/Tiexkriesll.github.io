95% Ice Puddle

Quickly places an ice puddle on a random target. These last a very long time, enough that you will have 3-4 up at all times.
Standing in this freezes you solid for a few seconds, important later.




85% Circles under everyone

The boss will place a giant donut on each player. This will consist of 2 explosions back to back, the outer giant circle followed by the inner part.
You need to press your iframe the moment the outer explosion happens to avoid the second hit.

Dps want to group up while the tank and the healer move just out of range.
Tank and healer can stack with dps if you choose, up to you.









In order to survive this laser, you need to quickly move into a nearby ice puddle to become frozen solid. This will make you immune to the damage.
In this clip, tank purposely moves to be furthest so its guaranteed to be the first laser target.


80% Knockup and Lasers

The boss will say "Your flesh will be deleted!" and raise his shield.
After about 1.5-2 seconds, he will cause an explosion, knocking anyone who doesnt iframe high into the air.

He will then follow this up with 2 very damaging lasers.
If anyone is hit by the knockup, they will be his target for the first laser.
If nobody is knocked up, the boss will target the furthest player for the first laser, followed by a random target for the second.

The boss will target someone and jump on them, stunning anyone near him if hit. Healers can remove this.

Curse Mechanic

The boss will say "Sacrificial Target selected" and cast either a Red or Blue aoe.
One person MUST get hit by this and receive a debuff from it.Taking the aoe again while you have the debuff will result in instant death.
If no one takes this, the boss will cast an aoe giving EVERYONE the same debuff, killing those who already have it.

You need to assign an order from 1-5, any order will do.
Tank - Healer - Dps - Dps - Dps anything will work.
Each person takes a debuff and then repeats.
In a dire situation, one person can take the debuff and die over and over if you need to recover.

70% Orb mechanic

When the boss teleports to the middle of the room, he will give every player a debuff: either white or black.
The boss can still be plagued here to slow it down if you choose.
After charging up, the boss will spawn 5 orbs around him.

In order to survive the explosion and not get stunned for 7 seconds, you need to kill the opposite orb of your debuff. In these clips the tank is assigned a black debuff in the top right.His job is to kill a white orb to receive the immunity buff.


50% Clones and teleport

The difference here is the attack based on position.
● If the boss teleports to the door, he will spin and so will the clones.
● If the boss teleports to the back wall, he will laser and so will the clones.

Unlike the cursed version, the lasers the clones shoot is NOT forked, it is single target like the boss

40% Cage

The boss will give every player a color debuff: Red, Blue, or Yellow.
If this debuff ever expires, you will instantly die.

You need to stand in the matching color on the floor to refresh it and add a stack.
Once you have 4 stacks of your assigned color, it will be removed without killing you.

At the same time, the boss will have a shield on him that you must break before the 4th cage cycle.
Dance around the boss matching your color while keeping the dps up.
