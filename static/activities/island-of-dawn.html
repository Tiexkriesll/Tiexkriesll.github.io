Island of Glowing Dawn

You can teleport to the Island of Glowing Dawn by an NPC located next to the respawn spot at North Dock.
He will teleport you right in the middle of North Dock at the Island of Glowing Dawn without any charge fee.

Reputation Quests

They are green quests in the North Dock - even though they do not reward any of the current reputation points they can reward you with extra Bronze Coins and Island of Dawn Conquest Badges daily.

You can accept these quests daily as long as you didnt finish your daily limit on Daily quests.


Island of Dawn Conquest Badge Shop

Island of Dawn Conquest Badges can be dropped by killing BAMs at the Island of Glowing Dawn. They are not party-loot, if farmed in party each member in party rolls for them.
