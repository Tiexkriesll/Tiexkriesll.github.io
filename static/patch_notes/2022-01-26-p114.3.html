*01/25 17:25 - Bug fixes have been updated.
*01/24 11:30 - Class balance has been updated.


Update

1. Class Mastery

  • Classes with mastery will also be able to use their inherited power.

    • By disabling all mastery skills, you can activate the inherited power effect.

      • The Inherited Power activation UI is added in the "Class Mastery" skill window.

      • When the Inherited Power is activated, it will be displayed on the Skill tab as [Passive].

2. Class Balance

  • Slayer

    • Spinning Delay (pl.)

      • Skill power increased as follows:

        • First hit : 5,632 -> 10,052

        • Second hit : 9,574 -> 10,052

        • Third hit : 12,953 -> 13,600

        • Slightly increased the chance of critical hit of 1st and 2nd hit.

    • Breaking Strike (pl.)

      • Skill power is increased by about 20%.

    • Blade Master: Giant Leap (pl.)

      • Changed that the power of the Breaking Strike skill increases from 20% -> 30%.

    • Blade Master: Breakdown (pl.)

      • Changed that the power of the Punishing Blow skill increases from 20% -> 30%.


3. Service stabilization

● To minimize server instability, overall server maintenance is carried out.




Bug Fixes

● Fixed issue with AWAKE tokens still present in Resonance Dungeon achievement rewards.
● Revised the problem when Energy of Seawater (active skill from Giant Hatch) is maintained when entering the battlefield.
● Revised the problem when a campfire is installed in the fishing area, fishing is canceled and cannot be executed again.
● Revised the problem that the costume effect does not activate when wearing the Hagufna's Cloak eligible for the effect activation.
● Revised the problem of Helen hitting one more time after the fourth attack in the Frozen Constellation dungeon storm.
● Revised drops of Exodor Field monsters which dropped Mahtnan related equipment and items.

TERA KR Exclusive

Regarding the "Frozen Constellation dungeon - Helen;problem of changing the pattern when the character aggro changes during the main pattern" fix during the maintenance on 1/19, many guardians have sent their worries and concerns to the Customer Center to conduct an internal investigation into the abuse act of this bug.
We conducted an internal investigation into the abusing behavior, but we ask for your understanding that it will be difficult to investigate the entire dungeon clear records as the investigation begins after some time has already passed.
As all Guardians strive for fair competition, we will do our best to prevent such problems from occurring in the future.
